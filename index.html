Crops and Livestock Commodities in Australia

Agriculture is an important part of Australia's economy. It accounts for around 11 per cent of goods and services trade, adds around 2.6 per cent of all employment, and exports goods to other countries as well. Farming in Australia can be broken down into two big sectors; Plant and Animal derivatives. These sectors fluctuate much from state to state, territory to territory, as much of the land in Australia is unarable, and different states having different conditions that would best fit to certain commodities.

Finally, inspecting the nine most populous LGAs, we find that there has been substantial improvement across the years. Across all nine LGAs, less than one in five students report having experienced bullying recently, a significant improvement.

Melbourne, in particular, has always had low bullying numbers, but has since been joined by Monash; both at one student in every eight.
